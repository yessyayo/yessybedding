ABOUT US

Welcome to Yessy Beddings, where comfort is at its peak! We are passionate about providing our customers with high-quality bedding products that are body-friendly and enhance sleeping in comfort. Yessy Beddings was founded by Ogunniranye Yesirat in 2020. Our journey began in 2020 with a simple idea to create beddings: bedsheets, duvets, bedcovers, duvet covers, and pillows that not only look great but also feel amazing. This business is dedicated to providing the highest quality bedding products to our customers.

MISSION

Our mission at Yessy Beddings is to provide our customers with a diverse selection of high-quality bedding products that cater to their unique preferences and lifestyle. We are committed to offering exceptional customer service, innovative designs, and sustainable practices that contribute to a better sleep experience and a more comfortable home environment.

VISION

At Yessy Beddings, we envision a world where every bedroom reflects comfort, style, and individuality. We strive to be the premier destination for quality bedding solutions that enhance the way you sleep, rest, and live.
